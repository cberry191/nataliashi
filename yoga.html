Classes

All classes are fully tailored to the student's skill level with a focus on anatomy and form.

Personalised instruction and advice are provided with an aim toward implementing your practice at home and making yoga a part of your regular routine.

Yoga For Beginners

Introductory classes for those new to yoga or looking to refine their practice. Learn the basic poses at a pace dynamically adjusted to your comfort level.

Private Yin + Sounds

A guided, deep experience of sound enhanced Pranayama breath work that triggers the body's relaxation response to enable healing and mitigate the effects of chronic stress.

Advanced + Intermediate Yoga

Classes with a focus on deepening practice and working towards more challenging Asanas and balances.

Everyday Mobility and Stretching

Non Yoga specific stretching and mobility routines suitable for seniors and athletes.

Nauli Kriya

Learn the advanced yogic cleansing exercise achieved via breathwork and rolling of the abdominal muscles. Must be practiced on an empty stomach.
